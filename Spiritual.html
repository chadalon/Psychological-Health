Spiritual Health

The spiritual dimension is the sense of belonging to something bigger than the physical or personal dimensions of existence. It includes a feeling of connection and unity with others. Religion can be a part of spiritual health, but it is not required to be a spiritual person.

There are many ways to improve spiritual health. Asking yourself questions like “Who am I?” “What is my purpose?” “What do I care about?” and others can lead you to more immersive thinking about your existence and figuring out what things help you feel more fulfilled. Looking for deeper meanings, focusing on the positive, and meditating can all greatly improve spiritual health.
