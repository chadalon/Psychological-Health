The Importance of Psychological Health

Psychological health includes four dimensions of health - the mental, emotional, social, and spiritual dimensions. People who are psychologically healthy have high energy, are focused, have a realistic sense of self and others, are sensitive to others and their environment, have healthy relationships and a strong social support, enjoy challenges, control tension and anxiety, and have good spiritual and intellectual health.
